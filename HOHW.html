HELL OR HIGH WATER


The Story so Far

Hell or High Water is a Management Pirate RPG game for PC. You as the player arrive on Traitor's Cove where you can assemble a crew and head out to the open seas for battle! The game is being developed inside the Unity engine, written in C#. I am no artist; the art was purchased from Synty Studios. However, the models came with no colliders, animations, or scripts. This project is an example of bringing a still game to life!

Concepts Used:

  • Colliders / Physics
  • Navmesh Surface/ Navmesh Agent
  • Interactive GUI
  • Save Data via MySQL database
  • Mixamo/Mechanim Animations
