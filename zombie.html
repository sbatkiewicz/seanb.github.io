Zombie Survival


It's Alive!

Zombie Survival is a First-Person shooter game I developed in Unity with C#. It is an endless game where the player has to kill as many zombies as they can before being overwhelmed by the horde! Made primarily of free/standard assets, however, all scripting was done by me.

Concepts Used:

  • Colliders / Real-Time Physics
  • Navmesh Surface/ Navmesh Agent
  • Updated HUD
  • Dynamically scripted lighting effects
  • AI Scripting
